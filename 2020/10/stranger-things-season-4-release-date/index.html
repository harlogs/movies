Stranger Things (season 4)
The fourth season of the American science fiction horror television series Stranger Things, titled Stranger Things 4, was announced by Netflix in September 2019. The fourth season will continue to be produced by the show’s creators, the Duffer Brothers, along with Shawn Levy, Dan Cohen, and Iain Paterson. The show’s main cast will continue to consist of Winona Ryder, David Harbour, Finn Wolfhard, Millie Bobby Brown, Gaten Matarazzo, Caleb McLaughlin, Noah Schnapp, Sadie Sink, Natalia Dyer, Charlie Heaton, Joe Keery, Maya Hawke, Priah Ferguson, and Cara Buono, with Brett Gelman promoted to series regular.

Production on the season was halted in March 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic, leaving its release date to be determined.
